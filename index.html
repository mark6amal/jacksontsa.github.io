TSA Motto

The TSA Chapter at Henry M. Jackson High School has been one of the largest and most successful clubs in all aspects. From teamwork, to friendship, to technology our Henry M. Jackson High School’s TSA Chapter embraces the TSA Motto of “Learning to Lead in a Technical World” to its fullest extent. Our TSA chapter intends to prepare all the future doctors, engineers, mathematicians, and scientists among our society to truly strive for their life dreams in the best manner possible: through learning, leadership, cooperation, and ambition.
